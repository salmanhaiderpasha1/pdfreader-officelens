Privacy Policy – PDF Reader: PDF Viewer App

Last Updated: January 2026

Introduction

At Office Lens: Android Tool, Translate, Scanner, PDF, your privacy is our top priority. Our PDF Reader: PDF Viewer App (the “App”) is designed to provide seamless scanning, viewing, and editing of PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and text documents. By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect multiple types of information to provide the App, ensure security, display ads, analyze performance, and improve user experience.

1. Information You Provide Directly

When you scan, upload, or edit a document, the App processes your files and data. If you log in using Google Sign-In, we may collect your name, email address, and profile photo. We do not access your Google password.

2. Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect information such as device type, operating system version, IP address, advertising ID, app usage patterns, and crash reports. This data helps us improve the App, optimize performance, and show relevant ads.

3. Sensitive Permissions

The App requires camera access strictly for scanning documents. The camera is never used in the background without your permission.

How We Use Your Information

  • Core functionality: scanning, viewing, and editing documents
  • Authentication: enabling Google Sign-In
  • Analytics & Performance: crash reports and feature improvement
  • Advertising & Marketing: serving and analyzing ads
  • Security: fraud prevention and abuse detection

Third-Party Services and Sharing

We share data with trusted third-party services for analytics and advertising. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob: https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/

We never sell your personal data. Shared data is used strictly for App operation, performance improvement, and ad personalization.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary. Files remain on your device unless you choose to upload or share them. Analytics and crash data are retained to improve the App. You may request deletion of personal information at any time by contacting mindmovertech@gmail.com.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a child has provided information, contact us for removal.
